Frequently Asked Questions

What is Sermon Keeper?

An iOS app that records sermons, adds timestamps to your notes, and gives you automatic transcription with summary.

Is Sermon Keeper free?

You get 3 free recordings. Then $6.99/month or $64.99/year for unlimited.

What languages are supported?

English and Spanish — both the app and AI transcription.

What Bible version is used?

King James Version (KJV) for now. More translations coming soon.

Can I share my notes?

Yes — export via email or plain text.

Best app to record sermons on iPhone?

Sermon Keeper is built specifically for church — with timestamped notes, Bible verses, and AI summaries.

Will I lose my recordings?

No — everything syncs to iCloud automatically.

Do I need an account?

No sign-up required. Just download and start recording.

Is my data secure?

Yes — all recordings and notes stay on your device and sync only to your personal iCloud. We don't have access to your data.
